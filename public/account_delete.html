Account & Data Deletion Request

At BatProx, we value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and permanently remove your personal data from our servers, you can do so using either of the methods below.

Method 1: Delete via the BatProx App (Quickest)

You can instantly delete your account directly from within the application. This process is automated and immediate.

  1. Open the BatProx app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab from the bottom menu.
  3. Scroll down and tap on Delete My Account.
  4. Confirm your action in the pop-up dialog.

Once confirmed, your account, wallet details, and task history will be permanently deleted.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email (Web Request)

If you no longer have the app installed and wish to delete your account, you can submit a deletion request via email. Our support team will process your request within 3-5 business days.

Please send an email from the address/phone number associated with your account with the following details:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - BatProx
  • Body: Please include your registered Full Name and Phone Number.
Send Deletion Email
⚠️ Important Note: Account deletion is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all your portal data, task history, and wallet balance. We will permanently wipe your personal information (Name, Phone Number, and transaction logs) from our active database, except where retention is required by law.
